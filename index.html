Crop Recommendation Inputs




Nitrogen (N): Helps plants grow strong and green. It affects leaf and stem growth.
Phosphorus (P): Supports root development and helps crops mature properly.
Potassium (K):Improves plant health, disease resistance, and overall crop quality.
pH Level: Shows how acidic or alkaline the soil is. Different crops grow better in different pH ranges.
Rainfall: The amount of water your field receives. It affects how well crops can grow.
Temperature: The heat level in your area. Some crops prefer cool climates, others need warmer conditions.
Humidity: The moisture in the air. It influences plant growth and disease risk.
